About Us

Learn more about the College Clubs Portal and our mission.

Who We Are

The BVM College Clubs Portal is a one-stop platform for students to discover, explore, and join various clubs that operate within our college. Whether you are passionate about technology, arts, sports, or public speaking, there is a club waiting for you.

Our clubs are student-led organisations that conduct workshops, competitions, seminars, and social events throughout the academic year. They provide an excellent opportunity to develop skills, build a network, and have fun outside of the classroom.

Our Mission

We believe every student deserves a platform to explore their interests and grow beyond academics. The portal aims to:

  • Make club information easily accessible to all students.
  • Promote upcoming events and activities across all clubs.
  • Encourage collaboration between different clubs.
  • Help students find communities that match their passion.

Quick Facts

  • 8+ active clubs across multiple disciplines.
  • 20+ events organised every academic year.
  • 500+ students participating in club activities.
  • Partnerships with industry leaders for workshops and talks.
